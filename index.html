About Us

R&S Studio is a collaborative music production house specializing in immersive game soundtracks, animation scores, and high-energy arrangements. We offer a full-scale production pipeline, transforming raw inspirations into industry-standard auditory experiences.

Composition and emotional depth are driven by Safara, who leverages over 10 years of improvisational piano and arrangement experience to craft the studio’s melodic core. Her deep understanding of music theory and soul-stirring piano foundations provides the essential "spark" for every project we undertake.

Leading the technical architecture is Rouri, a producer with 4 years of intensive experience in professional arrangement, precision mixing, and mastering. Specializing in J-Pop, Hyperpop, and aesthetic electronic textures, Rouri has cultivated a community of over 6,000 followers. He ensures that every track—from the initial beat to the final master—reaches a polished, world-class standard, blending modern "Abibi" vibes with cinematic intensity.

Collaborations: We are deeply passionate about aesthetic and adventurous indie projects. If you are building a visually stunning game or an ambitious animation that resonates with our style, we are open to pro bono (free) collaborations for the right teams. Let’s create something wonder-filled together.
