About Us

R&S Studio is a two-person music production team. Safara handles composition and melody writing, with a strong foundation in music theory and years of piano experience. Rouri focuses on arrangement, mixing, and mastering, with 4 years of production experience and 6,000+ followers across platforms.

Together, we cover the full music production pipeline — from original melodies to polished final tracks. Our styles range from J-pop and hyperpop to ambient and cinematic adventure scores.

We're open to pro bono collaborations for projects that resonate with us. If you're building something special, let's talk.
